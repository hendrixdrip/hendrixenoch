Professional Summary

Arubu Enoch is a creative freelance web designer and social media marketer based in Nigeria, specializing in helping startups and growing businesses build a powerful digital presence. With a strong eye for design and a results-driven mindset, Arubu delivers end-to-end digital solutions — from crafting custom, responsive websites to running targeted social media campaigns that grow audiences and drive real business results.

Arubu's approach combines modern design principles with practical business thinking, ensuring every project not only looks great but performs effectively in the real world. He works closely with clients to understand their brand, goals, and audience before crafting tailored digital solutions.
